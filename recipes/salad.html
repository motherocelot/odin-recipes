Delicious Side Salad

Tasty side salad for those of you who are too good for pizza

Ingredients

  • Mixed Leafy Greens
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Red onion

Dressing

  • EVOO
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Dijon Mustard

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and toss with vinaigrette to taste when ready to serve.
